Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free Personal plan?

The Personal plan includes everything you need to manage up to 3 vehicles: scheduling, basic contracts, and email support. You only pay $2.50 per completed rental.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes, all paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

How does the per-rental fee work?

For Personal and Starter plans, we charge a small fee per completed rental. This is deducted automatically or billed monthly, whichever you prefer.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and can arrange invoicing for Enterprise customers.
