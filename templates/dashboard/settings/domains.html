How Custom Domains Work

  1. Add your custom domain above (e.g., rentals.yourcompany.com)
  2. Add the TXT record to your DNS for verification
  3. Click "Verify" once the DNS record is active
  4. Add the CNAME record pointing to {{ base_domain }}
  5. SSL will be automatically provisioned (may take a few minutes)
  6. Your rental site will be accessible at your custom domain!
