# W-034: FleetFlow Marketing Landing Page

## Overview

Main marketing landing page for fleetflow.com (the platform domain, not tenant subdomains).
Markets FleetFlow to two audiences:
1. **Car owners** who want to rent out their vehicles (become FleetFlow tenants)
2. **Renters** who are looking for cars to rent (find local rental companies)

## Marketing Strategy

### Two Audiences, One Page

**Left Column - Car Owners (Primary Revenue)**
- Clear value prop: "Turn vehicles into income"
- Emphasize FREE tier to lower barrier
- Show pricing transparency upfront
- Email capture for lead generation
- Benefits focus: manage fleet, get bookings, collect payments

**Right Column - Renters (Secondary, Network Effect)**
- Search functionality to find local FleetFlow tenants
- Drives traffic to tenant sites
- Creates marketplace effect
- Popular locations as quick links

### Section Flow (Psychology)

1. **Hero** - Immediate clarity on what FleetFlow is
2. **Two Columns** - Self-selection: "Which am I?"
3. **How It Works** - Reduce friction, show simplicity (3 steps)
4. **Pricing** - Transparency builds trust, FREE tier converts
5. **Social Proof** - Testimonials overcome objections
6. **Features** - Details for those still researching
7. **Referral CTA** - Viral loop, "know someone" hook
8. **Final CTA** - Clear action for decided visitors

### Referral Coupon System

**Mechanics:**
- $25 credit for referred user
- $25 credit for referrer (when referral activates)
- Tracks "has cars" vs "looking for rental"
- Stored as `ReferralCredit` model

## Implementation Notes

### Files Created
- `apps/marketing/` - Django app for marketing pages
- `apps/marketing/models.py` - LeadCapture, ReferralCredit models
- `apps/marketing/views.py` - HomePageView, PricingView, FeaturesView, RentalSearchView
- `apps/marketing/forms.py` - SignupLeadForm, ReferralForm, RentalSearchForm
- `templates/marketing/` - Base template, home, search_results, pricing, features

### URL Structure
- `/` - Marketing homepage
- `/pricing/` - Detailed pricing page
- `/features/` - Features overview
- `/search/` - Rental company search
- `/api/lead/` - Lead capture endpoint (AJAX)
- `/api/referral/` - Referral submission endpoint (AJAX)

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Desktop: > 1024px (full two-column layout)
- Tablet: 768px - 1024px (stacked columns)
- Mobile: < 768px (single column, simplified)
